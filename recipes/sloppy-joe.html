Sloppy Joe

Description

A sloppy joe is a sandwich consisting of ground beef, onions, tomato sauce or ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, and other seasonings, served on a hamburger bun. There are several theories about the sandwich's origin.

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Manwich Original Sloppy Joe Sauce
  • Brioche Buns
  • 1lb of Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Thoroughly cook 1lb of ground beef.
  2. Stir in 1 can of Manwich Original Sloppy Joe Sauce
  3. Get asked to become a chef at a Michelin star restaurant.
  4. Serve on toasted brioche bun.
