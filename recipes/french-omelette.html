French Omelette

Description

French omelettes are cooked just until set, so they have a creamy, custard-like texture and no brown spots outside. They are rarely filled with anything other than a small amount of cheese, and they're rolled into a log rather than folded in half for serving.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Herbs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in the skillet, then add the beaten eggs.
  2. Gently loosen the omelette from the skillet, then fold and roll it into a log.
  3. Plate, butter the top, and scatter salt and herbs over the top. ENJOY!
